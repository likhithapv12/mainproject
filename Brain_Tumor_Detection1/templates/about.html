About the System

Our mission is to revolutionize early brain tumor detection.

Our Mission

The Brain Tumor Detection System uses state-of-the-art deep learning technology to analyze retinal images for signs of papilledema, a key indicator of increased intracranial pressure which can be caused by brain tumors.

By providing a fast, secure, and highly accurate analysis tool, we aim to assist medical professionals in the early identification of potential risks, leading to timely and effective treatment.

The Team

Developed by a dedicated team of KSSEM Final Year Students, this system combines expertise in computer vision, machine learning, and clinical diagnosis to deliver reliable results.

Our Commitment

We are committed to patient privacy and data security. All uploaded images and analysis results are handled with the utmost care, adhering to strict privacy protocols and compliance standards like HIPAA.
